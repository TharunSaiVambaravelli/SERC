About us?

Software Engineering Research Center(SERC) has a eminent faculty with vast teaching and research experience in and outside India. Some of the key focus areas:.

  1. Empirical Research and Process Improvement
  2. Formal Methods in Software Engineering
  3. Requirements Engineering and Quality Attributes
  4. Complex System Development
  5. Cloud Computing
  6. Education Technologies
  7. Usability Engineering

Venkatesh Choppella

Venkatesh Choppella is Associate Professor at the International Institute of Information Technology Hyderabad, India. He was also associated with Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management – Kerala, Trivandrum, and is currently adjunct faculty at IIIT Bangalore.He has held research and engineering positions in Hewlett Packard, Xerox Palo Alto Research Centre, Oak Ridge National Laboratory, and the Ohio State University in the United States.He holds a PhD. degree in Computer Science from Indiana University,USA, and Bachelors and Masters degrees from the Indian Institutes of Technology, Kanpur and Madras, respectively.

Vasudeva Varma

Vasudeva Varma is a Professor and the Dean (Research) at IIIT Hyderabad. His research interests include search , information extraction, information access, cloud computing and software engineering. He heads the Search and Information Extraction Lab at IIIT Hyderabad. He is the CEO of IIIT Hyderabad Foundation, which runs one of the largest technology incubators in India. He is the CEO of IIIT Hyderabad Foundation, which runs one of the largest technology incubators in India. He published a book on Software Architecture and close to two hundred technical papers in journals and conferences.In 2007, he was given Research Faculty Award by AOL Labs.

Research

To lead the software engineering research in India by addressing India specific issues in an industry driven environment.The SERC is a research and development center at IIIT Hyderabad conducting research in various domains of software engineering. The main objectives of SERC are to promote quality software development at universities through University Software Development Center and to operate closely with the industry by estabilishing touch points.
